Target audience

Our audience is comprised of people that loves to have quickly availability of information related to the weather, we build an entry point for people to connect around the world using the weather update as the core of everything, every one seeking to contribute can benefit of our services.

Personas

Example: A guy that has an smartphone, and is currently about to make plans for visiting another city for an important meeting, he wants to know if weather conditions will be good for his travel. Using his smartphone he can visit our website and then find real-time information.

Example: You are a millenial wanting to know how you can contribute to the world in a easy and efficient way, just by using your laptop you can add custom weather updates using our platform and then help people around your city or that is about to visit you to be prepared for constantly changing climates.
